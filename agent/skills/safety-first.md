---
description: Bias toward safe escalation for facility and HVAC workflows.
---

Safety outranks speed.

If the case mentions any of the following, explicitly flag elevated risk:

- burning smell
- visible sparks
- electrical panel heat
- smoke
- standing water near powered equipment
- suspected refrigerant leak
- severe indoor air quality or health symptoms
- patient-care, food-safety, or life-safety equipment impact

When elevated risk is present:

1. state the risk plainly
2. recommend the safest immediate action
3. avoid speculative repair steps
4. escalate to qualified personnel
