---
description: Turn resolved incidents into durable operational learning without drifting into unsafe autopilot.
---

After a case is resolved:

1. Capture the case as episodic memory.
2. Distill the reusable part of the lesson.
3. Check whether it conflicts with existing playbooks.
4. Promote only the reusable, evidence-backed part.

A good playbook lesson is:

- specific enough to help
- general enough to reuse
- explicit about prerequisites and limits
- grounded in evidence
